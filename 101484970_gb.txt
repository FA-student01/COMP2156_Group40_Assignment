George brown college is a public college which is located in the heart of Toronto.

Added information - GBC offers a wide range of diploma, degree, and certificate programs in business, technology, design, health sciences, hospitality, and more.

GBC has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront.

The college provides over 160 programs, including diplomas, degrees, and certificates.

GBC is known for its hands-on learning approach with co-op and internship opportunities.

The School of Computer Technology offers programs in software development, cybersecurity, and DevOps.

Many GBC graduates work in top companies like Google, IBM, Microsoft, and Shopify.
