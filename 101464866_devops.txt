DevOps For System Administration also known as COMP 2156 is a course that is very integral to
George Brown's Computer System Technician and Technology programs due to the core data types
and formats learned through out the course like JSON, XML, YAML and YANG. DevOps also allows
users to enhance their value in network automation while also introducing DevOps tools like
Git and Ansible. Students who complete this course will be able to carry out the role of network
engineer using Industry-standard tools like CI/CD. Students who take this course will have to
complete and submit weekly labs, take weekly quizes, while also being expected to complete
2 exams and a group and personal assignment.
