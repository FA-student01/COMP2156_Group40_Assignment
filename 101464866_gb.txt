George Brown College also known as The George Brown College of Applied Arts and Technology
is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The College was established in 1867
during the formation of Ontario's community college system. George Brown currently has
3 campuses and a plethora of diploma and degree programs to choose from. In the recent years
George Brown has also created online degrees and diplomas, so that students can pursue their
education virtually no matter where they are.
