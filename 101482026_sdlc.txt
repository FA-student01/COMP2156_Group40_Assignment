Initial Thoughts on Importance of devops in sdlc
DevOps enhances the SDLC by integrating development and operations, promoting collaboration, and automating processes. It speeds up delivery, improves quality, and enables continuous feedback, resulting in more efficient software development and deployment cycles.
evOps is vital in the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) as it integrates development and operations, fostering collaboration and streamlining processes. By automating workflows and enabling continuous integration, testing, and delivery, DevOps accelerates software releases, enhances quality, ensures reliability, and incorporates security. This approach drives innovation while meeting customer needs efficiently.
