What is the Importance of learning DevOps in the software development cycle? 

Description:
DevOps will help to speed up and secure software delivery though process like automation,
better collaboration, less errors, better efficiency and smoother software deployment workflow.
The DevOps approach helps teams adapt to changes faster, improve system security overall and helps with maintaining software through the development cycle.
Ultimately, DevOps creates an environment of continuos improvement thus making developed software more resilient and agile,
this approach ensures end users are recieving the highest quality software that meets all their needs.
